Valves

About Valves

Valves are mechanical devices used to control the flow of fluids within a system. They come in various types, such as gate valves, globe valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, and check valves. Each type serves a specific purpose, from regulating flow rate to preventing backflow.

Product Offerings

  • Valves control the rate of fluid flow within a system, allowing operators to adjust flow rates as needed.
  • Valves can completely stop the flow of fluid when closed, providing a means to isolate sections of a system for maintenance or emergency situations.
  • Certain valves, like check valves, ensure that fluid flows in only one direction, preventing backflow and maintaining system integrity.

Why Valves

Valves are crucial components in hydraulic and pneumatic systems, serving to control the flow of fluids or gases. They enable operators to regulate fluid flow rates, direct the flow in specific directions, and shut off the flow entirely when necessary.
